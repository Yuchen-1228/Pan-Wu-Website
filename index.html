"COLLEGE, COMMUNITY, CONNECTIVITY"




“Success is not final, failure is not fatal.

What lies in the future is what’s on your mind in the present”










As our sophomores continue into their junior year,
there is the question of colleges. One of our main goals this year is to offer a guide
for students to explore. This track will deliver the possibilities of what a student hopes
to accomplish in the future. It’ll also serve as a guide to what colleges and resources a
student is available to utilize. These clear, direct, and simplified tracks will allow the
student body to gain a head start on the college application process. It’ll help develop
and amplify the chances of admission.



Despite the enormous amount of resources available to our students,
there are still many unaware or unable to utilize them. We hope to create a school community
where everyone is engaged. We want to offer help: additional financial aid to student
projects on top of current funding. We also want to introduce more student events to
promote social engagements and divert from the stressful Stuy environment.



Our policies this year are mainly oriented towards opportunities.
We’ll connect students with those experienced alumni who will offer aid. We’ll prepare
students to assert independence and make choices in their best interests: programs,
internships, and other job opportunities. And help them secure these opportunities.
We hope to bring a closer network between those who know and those who don’t. We want to
build a body with solid foundations for college applications during senior year and the
rest of their high school career.
